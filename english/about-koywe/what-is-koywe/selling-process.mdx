Now you can use Koywe to sell your cryptos the easy way! The steps to use the Widget are very simple:

### 1. Select the crypto and fiat you wish to operate with.

<Frame>
  <div className="flex space-x-2">
    <img src="/images/user-validation-2.png" />
    <img src="/images/user-validation-3.png" />
  </div>
</Frame>

### 2. Input the amount you want to sell, or the amount you want to receive.

![](/images/monto.png)

<Info>
  At any time during the process you'll be able to see exactly how much you are
  paying and how much you'll receive, along with the comission details when
  clicking on **see details**.
</Info>

![](/images/detalle.png)

### 3. Verify your identity.

The steps for this process are available in the section ["User's validation"](/english/what-is-koywe/user-validation).

### 4. Select the bank account you want us to send the currency amount to.

<Frame>
  <div className="flex space-x-2">
    <img src="/images/selling-process-1.png" />
    <img src="/images/selling-process-2.png" />
  </div>
</Frame>

You can select an existing bank account or add a new one.

![](/images/selling-process-3.png)

### 5. Proceed with the payment.

![](/images/selling-process-4.png)

Send your cryptos to the address on the screen. You can copy the address or scan the QR code to do so.

![](/images/selling-process-5.png)

### 6. Check your transaction's status

To check your transaction's status, click the button that will be displayed under the QR code.

![](/images/selling-process-6.png)
